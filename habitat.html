The Hoh Rainforest Flying Tree Stingray (Himantura Polycryptus) is an amphibian in the class miyloamphibia.

Diet

The HRFTS an omnivorous creature. While scientists do not know the complete diet of the HFTS, they know that it feeds primarily on the Hoh Rainforest Tree Octopus, frogs and many differnt species of mushrooms.

Habitat

Flying Tree Stingray's Habitat is located exclusivley in the Hoh rainforest. Fortunatley, for the elusive stingray, The Hoh Rainforest is a national treasure that belongs in the Olympic National park. The HRFTS are territorial animals and compete for large areas of land to sustain them.
